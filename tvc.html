Thrust Vector Control Rocket

Introduction

While browsing YouTube one day, I came across a channel called BPS.Space.

At the time (the channel does many rocket-things now), it was about building model rockets which could stabalize themselves using Thrust Vector Control (TVC).

Thrust Vectoring is when a vehicle (like a rocket or plane) manipulates the direction of its thrust to change its direction and angular velocity.

The channel inspired me to build my own TVC rocket. I always liked space-related things, like rocketry, as well as engineering and software development. Building a rocket like this would include aspects of all of these, from PCB and engine mount design to writing stabalization software, so it seemed like the perfect project. This page is a documentation of my progress on this project, and will be updated periodically.
